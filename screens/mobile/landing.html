How it works

Four layers. One pass.

Defacta does not simply say true or false. It runs every input through four layers of structured analysis and returns one traceable verdict.

Layer 01

Factual verification

Claims checked against credible sources and authoritative databases. Hallucinations and fabricated citations detected and flagged.

Layer 02

Source credibility

Every source evaluated for reliability, track record, and relevance. Anonymous or low-credibility sources identified.

Layer 03

Bias and manipulation

Manipulative framing, selective omission, and persuasion tactics detected. Bias assessed and scored across dimensions.

Layer 04

Misinformation registry

Cross-referenced against fact-checking registries and known misinformation databases. Every verdict timestamped and traceable.

Mission

We do not replace human judgment. We protect it.

Defacta is built on one premise: analysts, journalists, fact-checkers, and researchers are irreplaceable. We build tools that make their work faster, not tools that replace it.

Defacta handles the mechanical parts so human attention goes where it matters. A limited but sufficient version is free for anyone who wants to know what they are reading.

Defacta makes no claim to ultimate truth. It reduces the risk of being misled, manipulated, or gaslighted.
