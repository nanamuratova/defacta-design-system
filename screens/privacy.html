Privacy Policy

Last updated · June 17, 2026

This policy explains what Defacta collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to defacta.pro and the Defacta apps. See also our Terms of Service.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data — name, email, and authentication details.
  • Content you submit — the text and links you ask Defacta to verify.
  • Usage data — how you use the service, device and log information.
  • Billing data — handled by our payment processor; we do not store full card numbers.

2. How we use information

We use your information to provide the verification you request, maintain your account and history, process payments, secure the service, and communicate with you. We process content only to produce the report you asked for.

3. Your content and model training

Your documents are private by default and are not used to train models. We do not sell your content. Verification is performed to return your results, and content is retained only as described below.

4. Legal bases

Where the GDPR applies, we process personal data to perform our contract with you, to comply with legal obligations, and for our legitimate interests in operating and improving the service — balanced against your rights.

5. Sharing & disclosure

We share data with service providers who process it on our behalf (such as hosting and payments) under appropriate safeguards, and when required by law. We do not sell personal data.

6. Data retention

We keep account data while your account is active and reports for as long as your plan provides history. You can delete reports at any time; we remove or anonymise data when it is no longer needed, subject to legal requirements.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to run the service and limited analytics to understand usage. You can control non-essential cookies through the consent options provided.

10. Contact

Questions or requests about privacy? Email legal@defacta.pro.
