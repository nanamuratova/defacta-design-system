How it works

Six steps. No magic

Defacta does not simply say true or false. It breaks long-form material into a review structure that expert teams can use in real work.

Step 1

Ingest

Paste a URL, document, or raw model output.

Step 2

Extract

Defacta atomises the text into discrete claims.

Step 3

Verify

Each claim is checked against sources and evidence.

Step 4

Flag

Risks, hallucinations, and uncertainty are surfaced.

Step 5

Report

A structured, exportable report is generated.

Step 6

Review

A human reviewer signs off and locks the record.
